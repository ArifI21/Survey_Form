What Are Game Reviews?

Game reviews just like films, products and basically everything, have reviews so that people can gain an understanding of what the game is like to those people who have played the game. They have a rating, usually from 0 to 5 and they have a comment stating why it deserves that rating. They can be very useful to those who are unsure about whether they should buy a certain game. You can read the reviews for the game and you can then decide whether you should buy it or not.
