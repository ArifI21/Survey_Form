Timeline and events that occurred in Nelson Mandela's life

• Nelson Mandela was born in Mvezo, South Africa in 1918

• He completed his BA through the University of South Africa (UNISA) in 1942

• In 1962, Mandela was sentenced to five years in prison for incitement and leaving the country without a passport

• He was elected by Parliament as first president of a democratic South Africa in 1994

• Mandela steps down after one term as President and establishes the Nelson Mandela Foundation

• His book 'Nelson Mandela By Himself: The Authorised Book of Quotations' is launched

• Mandela died in Johannesburg, South Africa in 2013 aged 95
